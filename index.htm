Powells Music Shop, also known as The Four Corners, has been serving customers in Galway since 1918. Over the past 100 years we have evolved and changed but our priority has always been to promote Irish Musical Culture and to offer a unique customer experience.

The passionate, friendly and expert Powells team is always ready to guide you through our huge selection of Music Instruments and Accessories whether it’s Traditional, Modern, Jazz or Classical. We deal with some of the world’s top brands and have something for every musician at every level. Be sure not to miss our amazing Special Offers and Second Hand Instrument sales. And if you’re far from Galway, give us a call: We ship worldwide!

There’s also lots of things to discover in our shop: a great range of Irish Music CDs, Music Books, Irish Gifts and Souvenirs, Art Supplies and Stationery, Walking Sticks and Smoking Pipes, you name it! Last but not least, we do Instrument Rentals, Specialised Repairs and selected Trade-ins.
